# 🎯 Focus Bubbles

Because nothing says "I have my life together" quite like dragging circles around a screen and pretending they represent your priorities.

Focus Bubbles is a single-page bubble chart for visualising and adjusting the things you *say* matter to you. Add bubbles, resize them, drag them around — basically a vision board for people who think vision boards are cringe but still need one.

## Features

- **Add focus areas** as bubbles (work, health, "finally learning guitar", etc.)
- **Resize them** relative to each other — watch "sleep" shrink in real time
- **Drag them around** — because spatial arrangement definitely reflects your inner truth
- **Edit labels** by double-clicking — for when "side hustle" becomes "coping mechanism"
- **Persistent storage** — your delusions are saved to localStorage so they survive a refresh
- **PWA-ready** — add it to your home screen and pretend it's a real app

## Usage

Open `index.html` in a browser. That's it. It's one file. We're not animals.
